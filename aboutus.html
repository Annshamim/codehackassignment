WHO WE ARE

Pwani Teknowgalz is a forward-thinking coding school committed to empowering young women in the coastal region with coding and technology skills. Dedicated to bridging the gender gap in the tech industry, Pwani Teknowgalz provides hands-on training, mentorship, and a supportive community, creating a pathway for aspiring female coders to excel and thrive in the world of technology.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving world of technology. We are committed to providing an inclusive and accessible learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Our Vision:

We envision a future where every individual has the opportunity to unleash their potential through coding education. Our vision is to be a global leader in providing transformative learning experiences that empower people to thrive in the digital age.
