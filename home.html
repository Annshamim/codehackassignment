WHO WE ARE

Pwani Teknowgalz is a forward-thinking coding school committed to empowering young women in the coastal region with coding and technology skills. Dedicated to bridging the gender gap in the tech industry, Pwani Teknowgalz provides hands-on training, mentorship, and a supportive community, creating a pathway for aspiring female coders to excel and thrive in the world of technology.

Learning

At Pwani Teknowgalz school, we take pride in fostering a cutting-edge educational environment that equips students with essential skills for the future. Our comprehensive curriculum goes beyond traditional subjects, incorporating specialized programs in coding skills and digital marketing. In the coding skills program, students delve into Machine Learning and data science, Website and mobile development, preparing them for the dynamic tech landscape. Simultaneously, our digital marketing program empowers students with strategic insights into online promotion, social media management, and data analytics. With a commitment to innovation and practical knowledge, Pwani Teknowgalz School is shaping well-rounded individuals ready to thrive in the digital era.

Leasing Space

Leasing space in our school involves offering specific areas or facilities within our educational institution for external entities to use. This arrangement accommodates various needs such as hosting events, learning space, or community activities. Through negotiated terms, including rental duration and fees, we facilitate a mutually beneficial relationship, allowing external organizations to access our well-equipped spaces. This not only generates additional revenue for the school but also fosters community engagement by providing a venue for diverse activities. The leasing process ensures efficient utilization of our facilities while supporting the broader goals of our educational community.

Leasing Equipments

Leasing equipment in our school involves providing external parties access to our school's assets for a specified duration and agreed-upon terms. This arrangement enables organizations or individuals to benefit from our well-maintained and up-to-date equipment without the long-term commitment of ownership. Through this leasing process, we not only generate additional revenue for our school but also contribute to community collaborations and resource-sharing initiatives. The terms typically cover considerations such as lease duration, maintenance responsibilities, and fees, ensuring a transparent and mutually beneficial agreement. This approach supports the efficient use of our resources while fostering partnerships beyond the school's immediate community.
